A sorter for characters from the mobile game Tokyo Afterschool Summoners. Pick your sources, and hit the Start button.

Certain options have details that you can hover to read.

Click on the character you like better from the two, or tie them if you like them equally or don't know them.



There are 240+ named characters included in this sorter, 560+ in total.

Since completing the sorter will probably take a while, you may want to try:

・ Saving your progress and continuing at a later time.

・ Tying characters you don't have a strong preference over. This should reduce the amount of battles you'll sort through.

・ Using more filters than selected by default. This should reduce the amount of characters you'll sort through.



- Keyboard controls during sorting -

H or LeftArrow: Pick left ・ J or DownArrow: Undo ・ K or UpArrow: Tie ・ L or RightArrow: Pick right ・ S: Save progress

- Before sorting -

S or Enter: Start sorting ・ L: Load progress

1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons.
